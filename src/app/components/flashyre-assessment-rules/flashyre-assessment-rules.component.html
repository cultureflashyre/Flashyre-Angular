Corporate Skill-Based Assessment 
Rules for the Corporate Skill-Based Assessment 
Test Details:
The test consist of 5 multiple choice questions based on the Assessment skill.
The time limit for the test is 10 minutes.
Test Taking:
You must answer all 5 questions within the 10-minute time limit.
You can navigate between the questions and change your answers before submitting.
Once the 10-minute time limit is up, the test will automatically submit, even if you have not answered all the questions.
Scoring:
Each correct answer will be awarded 1 point.
Your final score will be displayed at the end of the test.
Important Notes:
The test will auto-submit after 10 minutes, regardless of whether you have answered all the questions or not.
Make sure to review your answers carefully before the time runs out.
No late submissions will be accepted.
Note:
1. Switching tabs or move to different application will lead to auto-submittion of this assessment.
2. This assessment session is being video recorded for monitoring and evaluation purpose.
Good luck!
